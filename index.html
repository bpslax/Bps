Introduction

The founders of Bhartiya Shikshan Sansthan, with a zeal and a visionary outlook, have demonstrated that anything worthwhile can be achieved through sincerity of purpose, right execution and implementation. Within a relatively short span of time, Bhartiya Shikshan Sansthan is today counted one of the best institutions in Rajasthan. Bhartiya Shikshan Sansthan is committed to providing world-class academics, infrastructure, and a highly disciplined atmosphere.
